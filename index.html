// 07 · straight answers

Questions, answered.

Do I need to install anything? +

No. It's a web app — sign in with an email magic link or Google and you're using it. No terminal, no setup script, no API keys.

Is the free plan actually free? +

Yes — 5 reel analyses a month, plus the library, chat, and strategy tools, with no card on file. Pro removes the limit when you're posting enough for it to matter.

What happens to my data? +

It's yours. Export everything as JSON from Settings any time, and delete your account (and all its data) yourself — no email to support required. The privacy policy is written in plain English.

Can I cancel Pro? +

Any time, in one click, from Settings → Manage billing. You drop back to the free plan and keep your whole library.

Will you keep updating it? +

Yes — it's the same system I run every day, and it's hosted, so new tools land in your account the moment they ship. Platform connects (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram) are rolling out now.
